📋 1. Abstract

FUTURE-eco-system is a Telegram-based tap-to-earn gaming ecosystem built on the TON blockchain. Players earn FUTURE tokens by tapping the Crystal Bee, completing daily missions, and building referral networks. The more the community grows — the sooner FUTURE becomes a real token on TON mainnet.

Every token earned now counts toward your Token Generation Event (TGE) allocation. Early players receive priority access and bonus multipliers when FUTURE launches on exchange.

FUTURE token is currently in-game only. Once the community reaches 50,000 active players, we will deploy the official FUTURE token on TON mainnet. Early players will receive priority access and bonus allocation during the TGE.
