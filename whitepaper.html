📋 1. Abstract

FUTURE-eco-system is a Telegram-based, tap-to-earn game built on the TON blockchain. Players earn in-game FUTURE tokens by tapping the Crystal Bee, completing daily missions, playing mini-games, collecting NFTs, and inviting friends.

FUTURE is an in-game token used inside the app (shop, breeding, games). It currently has no cash value and is not tradable. Our long-term goal is to grow the community and, if conditions allow, explore launching FUTURE as a real token on TON — but this is a plan, not a promise (see Roadmap & Disclaimers).

🎮 2. What You Can Do Today

The game is live now. Current features:

  • 👆 Tap-to-earn — tap the Crystal Bee that visually evolves through levels.
  • 🎁 Daily login (10-day cycle), daily missions, treasure chests.
  • 🎰 Mini-games — Coinflip, Slots, Crash, Mines, Lucky Spin.
  • 🐝💎 Crystal NFTs that generate a daily in-game yield.
  • 🎲 Weekly TON Lottery & 🍯 Hive Pot — buy in with TON; one winner takes a prize. Paid out on-chain.
  • 🧬 Bee breeding, 👥 referrals, 🏆 leaderboards, multi-language UI.

A shared Jackpot Pool is funded by in-game spending (shop, breeding, games) — nothing is burned. It is redistributed to the community. All TON payments flow through official TonConnect and are publicly verifiable on the TON blockchain.

🚀 3. Token Launch Plan (aspirational)

FUTURE is in-game only today. Our goal is to deploy FUTURE as a TON Jetton once the community reaches 100,000 active players — provided it is legally and technically feasible at that time. A larger community first means a healthier, fairer launch.
