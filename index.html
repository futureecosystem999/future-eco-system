📋 1. Abstract
FUTURE-eco-system is a Telegram-based tap-to-earn game built on the TON blockchain. Players earn in-game FUTURE tokens by tapping the Crystal Bee, playing mini-games, collecting NFTs, and inviting friends. FUTURE is an in-game token with no cash value today. Our long-term goal is to grow the community and, if conditions allow, explore launching FUTURE as a real token on TON — a plan, not a promise.
🚀 2. Token Launch Plan (aspirational)
FUTURE is in-game only today. Our goal is to deploy FUTURE as a TON Jetton once the community reaches 100,000 active players — provided it is legally and technically feasible at that time. A larger community first means a healthier, fairer launch.
ABOUT YOUR IN-GAME BALANCE
If a token launch ever happens, in-game activity may be considered when designing any future allocation. This is not a guarantee of tokens, value, or timing.
